How the enquiry process works

This static homepage highlights the core flow of the enquiry management system.

1. Submit

Users submit a new enquiry with contact, category, and issue details.

2. Review

Teams review the request, assign ownership, and update the priority.

3. Resolve

Each enquiry moves through status updates until it is completed.
